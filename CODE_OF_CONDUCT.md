# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone involved in this project and its community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being patient and understanding with other contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, bullying, or discriminatory language or actions
- Attacks on individuals or groups based on personal characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Threats of violence or violent language directed at another person
- Sustained disruption of community discussions
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Engaging in bad faith arguments or trolling

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including:

- GitHub repository (issues, discussions, pull requests)
- Project communication channels
- In-person or online project events
- Any other forums or communication channels used to coordinate the project

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face temporary or permanent removal from community spaces, at the discretion of project maintainers.

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have concerns, please contact the project maintainers privately:

- Email the maintainer at the contact provided in the SECURITY.md file
- Provide details about the incident and any supporting information

All reports will be handled with confidentiality and respect. Maintainers will investigate and take appropriate action.
